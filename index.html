animals in karura forest

By now i know you have heard of all sorts of scary snakes (they are not all over the place, so don't get scared and diffinately they cannot attack you unless you pose some sort of danger to them) in Karura Forest, that is if you live around the forests' surroundings or environ. But did you know, apart from the snakes of which most people tend to focus on, there are all sort of other animals in the forest such as colombus monkeys, deer, antelope, all sorts of bird among other creatures.
